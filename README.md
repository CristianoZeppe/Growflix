Growflix is a streaming-inspired web platform focused on the world of programming and technology. Instead of movies and TV shows, the platform delivers curated video content about software development, career growth, and real experiences from the tech industry.

The platform organizes content into different sections, such as Growcast episodes, webinars sessions, UX/UI and Developer Experiences, where professionals share their journeys, challenges, and insights from working in tech.

Built with a strong focus on clean layout, responsiveness, and user experience, Growflix recreates the look and feel of a modern streaming service while showcasing front-end development skills.
